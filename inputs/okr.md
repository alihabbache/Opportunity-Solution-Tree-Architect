## Objective
Increase weekly active users (WAU) by 20% by end of Q3 2025.

## Why This Matters
Our core growth metric is WAU. Currently at 8,500 WAU (baseline: June 1, 2025).
We need to reach 10,200 WAU by September 30, 2025.

## Key Results

- **KR1:** Reduce 7-day churn from 35% to 20%
  - Owner: Product / Growth
  - Current: 35% | Target: 20%

- **KR2:** Increase Day-7 retention from 40% to 55%
  - Owner: Product / Onboarding
  - Current: 40% | Target: 55%

- **KR3:** Grow core workflow completion rate from 30% to 50% of WAU
  - Owner: Product / Core Experience
  - Current: 30% | Target: 50%

## Success Definition
A user is considered "active" if they complete at least one core workflow action
(create, collaborate, or share) within a 7-day rolling window.
