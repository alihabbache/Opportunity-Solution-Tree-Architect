# Company Context — Mission, Vision & Product Goals

## Company Mission
Empower businesses of every size to operate with the speed and clarity of a startup —
by making enterprise-grade workflow software radically simple to adopt and use.

## Product Vision
Become the fastest-to-value business operations platform in the market.
Any team, any size, fully operational in under a week — without needing an implementation consultant.

## Core Product Principles
1. **Time-to-value above all** — every feature decision should ask: does this help customers see value faster?
2. **Self-serve first** — customers should be able to onboard, configure, and expand without human intervention.
3. **Progressive disclosure** — show the right information at the right time; never overwhelm a new user.
4. **Proactive guidance** — the product should tell users what to do next, not wait for them to figure it out.

## FY2025 Product Goals
- **Activation:** 75% of new customers complete core workflow within 5 days of signup (current: 42%)
- **Retention:** 80% 90-day retention for customers who onboard in under 5 days (current: 55%)
- **Expansion:** 40% of accounts expand to a second team within 6 months (current: 18%)
- **Support deflection:** Reduce onboarding-related support volume by 60% through in-product guidance

## Target Customer Segments
1. **SMB (10–100 employees):** Self-serve, price-sensitive, no dedicated IT. Need zero-friction setup.
2. **Mid-market (100–500 employees):** Mix of self-serve and assisted onboarding. Need integrations and SSO.
3. **Enterprise (500+ employees):** Dedicated implementation, complex permissions, compliance requirements.

## Current Competitive Landscape
- Main competitors prioritise features over onboarding experience
- Our key differentiator opportunity: fastest time-to-value in the category
- Customers who evaluate us against Competitor A cite onboarding complexity as the #1 reason they choose them
