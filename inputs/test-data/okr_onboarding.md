## Objective
Reduce customer onboarding time from 14 days to 5 days by end of Q4 2025.

## Why This Matters
Our current onboarding process is the #1 reason cited in churn interviews.
Customers who onboard in under 5 days have 3x higher 90-day retention.
Current average onboarding time: 14 days. Target: 5 days.

## Key Results

- **KR1:** Reduce median time-to-first-value from 14 days to 5 days
  - Owner: Product / Onboarding Squad
  - Current: 14 days | Target: 5 days

- **KR2:** Increase onboarding completion rate from 42% to 75%
  - Owner: Product / Growth
  - Current: 42% | Target: 75%

- **KR3:** Reduce onboarding-related support tickets by 60%
  - Owner: Customer Success / Product
  - Current: ~120 tickets/month | Target: ~48 tickets/month

- **KR4:** Achieve NPS of 40+ among users who completed onboarding in the last 30 days
  - Owner: Customer Success
  - Current: 22 | Target: 40

## Success Definition
A customer is considered "onboarded" when they have completed their first end-to-end
workflow using the core product feature with at least 2 team members active.
