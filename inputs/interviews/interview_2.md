# User Interview Notes — Session 2
**Date:** June 12, 2025
**Participant:** Marcus, Product Manager, new user (2 weeks)
**Interviewer:** Ali

---

## Opening

**Q: You signed up about two weeks ago. What was the first session like?**

"Honestly, confusing. I signed up, landed on a blank dashboard, and had no idea what to do. There was a tour popup but I dismissed it because I thought I'd figure it out. Big mistake."

**Q: Did you complete the tour eventually?**

"No. I just kind of clicked around. I created one project but I'm not sure I used it the right way."

---

## Pain Points

**Q: What made it hardest to get started?**

"There was no clear 'start here' moment. Like, what's the first thing I should do? What does 'done' look like for a new user? I didn't know."

"I also didn't understand the difference between a 'workspace' and a 'project'. The terminology was confusing."

**Q: Did you reach out for help?**

"I looked at the docs but they assumed I already knew the basics. I gave up after 5 minutes."

---

## Moments of Value

**Q: Was there a moment when something clicked?**

"Yeah, actually. A colleague showed me the collaboration feature in a meeting and I thought 'oh, that's what this is for'. That was day 4. Before that I had no idea that was even possible."

---

## Suggestions

**Q: What would have made week one better?**

"A simple checklist. Like: step 1, invite your team. Step 2, create your first project. Step 3, try the collaboration view. Just tell me what to do and I'll do it."

"Or a short video. Even 90 seconds. I'm a visual learner."
